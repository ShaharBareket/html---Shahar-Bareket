Visit this awesome Volcano site

Viarika, Pucon, Chile, Sep 24, 2020

Viarika, the "devil's house", is one of Chile's most active volcanoes, rising above the lake and town of the same name, 750 km (470 mi) south of Santiago.
It is the western most of three large strato volcanoes perpendicular to the Andean chain along the Mocha-Viarika Fault Zone.
Guided ascents are popular during summer months.
Villarrica, with its lava of basaltic-andesitic composition, is one of a small number worldwide known to have an active (but in this case intermittent) lava lake within its crater.
